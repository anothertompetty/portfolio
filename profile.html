How I work with startups

As a designer and consultant, I work with startups to conceive and create products that people love, and advise them on how their brand can become a platform for massive growth.

How I work with bigger companies

I help large, established businesses apply the best bits of startup thinking to find new markets, new business models and better ways of working.

This is done through a mix of innovation workshops and prototyping. When appropriate, I partner with the best people — developers, entrepreneurs and strategists — to complete larger projects.

When I have the time, I design and develop my own products and services.
