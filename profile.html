How I work

I work in a few different ways, depending on the goals of the project.

With startups

As a designer and consultant, I work with startups to conceive and create products that people love, and advise them on how their brand can become a platform for massive growth.

This is achieved through product strategy, UX and interface design, and brand identity.

With bigger companies

I help large, established businesses apply the best bits of startup thinking to find new markets, new business models and better ways of working.

This is done by mixing innovation workshops with rapid prototyping. When needed, I partner with the best people — developers, entrepreneurs, strategists — to complete larger projects.

With personal clients

I work directly with clients of all types, designing websites, apps and identites that drive growth and make users very happy indeed.
