GoCardless website

GoCardless makes recurring payments software for businesses. When I joined the focus was on small merchants (think gyms), and the product had been built to suit their needs. However, as the company grew, a new vision evolved: to build the recurring payments network for the internet. With a new vision came a desire to serve bigger, more demanding merchants (think telcos) with vastly different needs. A new, more robust approach was needeed.
