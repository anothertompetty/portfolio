Daysmiths

A curated behind-the-scenes guide to London with a personal touch

Launch site

Daysmiths is a site that tells you what to do with your day. I designed and built it primarily for myself since I never know what to do in my spare time.

It provides curated, complete days straight from real people, including their tips, must-sees and watch-outs. Every item has a unique action so you can view their route, check the menu, or book a ticket. Currently in beta, Daysmiths will be launching properly at the end of May.

For those interested in this sort of thing, Daysmiths is built to be fully responsive, compiled with Jekyll and powered by Github Pages
