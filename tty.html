Working together

I like working across a pretty broad range of things, but specialise in helping organisations work out what they should be building for their customers, and then partnering with them to bring those ideas to life.

The first part is a mix of uncovering insights and developing a vision. It means speaking to both customers and employees, prototyping ideas, and developing a framework that makes knowing what to create — and why — clear.

The second part takes the insights and vision and applies it to create new value for both customers and businesses. I might design a website or an app, or perhaps run workshops with employees. I can deliver large projects by partnering up with a small, talented network of developers, brand strategists, and content experts when necessary.

There's no one-size-fits-all project, so please feel free to email me to discuss working together.
